FAQ

What is pairs trading?

Pairs trading is an investment strategy used in all things stocks and cryptocurrency. It involves matching a long position (meaning that there is an expectation for the price to rise) with a short position (meaning that there is an expectation that the price is decreasing or will decrease) in two stocks with a high correlation. This allows traders to profit from any market condition: uptrend, downtrend, or sideways movement.

Why trade pairs?

Because of its market neutrality, this trading strategy can be safe and immune to any global market crisis, even if the entire market or sector falls down.
